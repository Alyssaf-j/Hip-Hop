MUS 307 Hip Hop Decoded - Early Hip Hop

Kool Herc "merry-go-round" technique

Students will identify DJs, their set-up and techniques that shaped early Hip-Hop. They will also be able to describe the social, economic, racialized and gendered dynamics of a Postindustrial NY that contributed to the formation of Hip-Hop:

  • Set-up: platter, pitch adjuster, start/stop, arm, needle
  • Beat breaks and techniques
  • 3 common beat breaks: ‘Amen’, ‘Funky Drummer’, ‘Apache’
  • Beat matching (BPM)
  • Merry-go-round (aka cut & mix) by DJ Kool Herc
  • Extending the break by Grandmaster Flash
